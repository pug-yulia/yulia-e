Rukeri


Description:

Black tea from volcanic soil in high altitude garden and hand-picked. African Black Tea from the Rukeri Valley. Composed of fine, whole Orange Pekoe leaves, and made by the orthodox method no longer commonly used. It is an un common tea, but with exceptional cultivation characteristics that give it an interesting flavour and character. A perfect breakfast tea, strong and rounded, ideal for lovers of Assam and Ceylon Black Teas. Malty flavour, with sweet nutty and fruity notes. Black tea is the tea variety with the highest theine content. Theine is a natural stimulant that relaxes the muscles, keeps you awake and clears the mind. It has a diuretic and antioxidant effect.

Cafeine level:

High

Preparation method:

4g 250ml 95ºC 2-4 min

Aroma:

Malty, with a sweet note

Flavour:

Malty, with a nutty and fruity finish. With marked astringency

Color:

Brown

Origin:

Ruanda, Africa
