Pu Ehr


Description:

Post-fermented tea with a light earthy taste. Until recently, it was unknown in the western world. It is achieving great popularity because it has been proved to help weight loss and also to reduce cholesterol levels. Ingredients:Red tea from the Pu’er region in Yunnan Province, China.

Cafeine level:

Medium-High, depends on brewing

Preparation method:

3-5g 250ml 90-95ºC 3-5 min

Aroma:

Earth, undergrowth

Flavour:

Earthly

Color:

Brown with red neuances

Origin:

Yunnan province, China
